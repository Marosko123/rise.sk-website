Deciding whether to hire a programmer or hire an agency? It's a common dilemma. However, a modern website is no longer a one-person job.

## The Myth of the "Full-Stack" Superhero
Finding one person who masters top-notch design (UX/UI), frontend (React/Next.js), backend, databases, servers (DevOps), and even SEO is almost impossible. And if they exist, they are extremely expensive.
With outsourcing, you get a **whole team of experts** for the price of one or two employees. You have a designer, programmer, tester, and project manager at your disposal.

## Flexibility and Speed
An internal employee can get sick, quit, or simply fall behind. An agency has redundancy. When you need to "hustle" before a project launch, the agency deploys more people. When the project is done and maintenance is running, you only pay for what you need.

## Know-how and Technologies
IT companies follow trends, train, and use the latest tools because it is their core business. Your internal programmer can easily "stagnate" on technologies from 5 years ago.

## When is an Internal Team Worth It?
If you are a technology company developing your own product (SaaS) which is the core of your business, then it makes sense to build an internal team. For most companies that need a website or e-shop to sell their products, outsourcing is more efficient and safer.
