PPC ads (Google Ads, Facebook Ads) are great for a quick start. But what happens when you stop paying? Traffic drops to zero. SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is a long run, but with the best return on investment (ROI).

## Paid vs. Organic Traffic
- **Paid:** You pay for every click. Prices are rising, competition is growing.
- **Organic:** You don't pay for clicks. People find you because you have relevant content. It's "free" (except for the investment in content creation and technical SEO).

## Technical SEO for E-shops
E-shops are specific for SEO. They have thousands of products, categories, and filters.
- **Category Structure:** Must be logical and shallow (max 3 clicks from the homepage).
- **Filters:** Watch out for duplicate content when filtering.
- **Speed:** Google loves fast e-shops.

## Content is King (Even in E-shops)
Copying the description from the supplier is not enough.
- **Unique Product Descriptions:** Write for people, not robots.
- **Blog:** Write guides, reviews, comparisons. It helps capture people in the decision-making phase.
- **FAQ:** Answer customer questions directly on the page.

Your own e-shop with quality SEO is an asset that no one can take away from you. Unlike a profile on a Marketplace, where you are just a tenant.
