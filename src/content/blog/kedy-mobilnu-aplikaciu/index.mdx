  ## Web vs. Aplikácia: Aký je rozdiel?

  - **Web:** Je skvelý na získavanie nových zákazníkov (cez Google). Je
  univerzálny a funguje všade.

  - **Aplikácia:** Je nástroj na udržanie si existujúcich zákazníkov (retencia).
  Ponúka lepší zážitok a funkcie, ktoré web nedokáže.


  ## Kedy ísť do aplikácie?

  1.  **Potrebujete offline režim:** Ak vaši používatelia potrebujú pracovať s
  dátami aj bez internetu (napr. technici v teréne, cestovatelia).

  2.  **Využívate funkcie telefónu:** GPS, kamera, gyroskop, Bluetooth. Web k
  nim má obmedzený prístup, aplikácia plný.

You have a great responsive website. Customers use it, orders are coming in. So why should you invest thousands of euros in a mobile app? The answer is not always straightforward, but there are situations where an app is a "game-changer".

## Web vs. App: What's the Difference?
- **Web:** Great for acquiring new customers (via Google). It is universal and works everywhere.
- **App:** A tool for retaining existing customers (retention). It offers a better experience and features that the web cannot.

## When to Go for an App?
1.  **You Need Offline Mode:** If your users need to work with data even without the internet (e.g., field technicians, travelers).
2.  **You Use Phone Features:** GPS, camera, gyroscope, Bluetooth. The web has limited access to them, the app has full access.
3.  **You Want to Send Push Notifications:** "Your item has been shipped" or "We have a discount for you". Notifications have a much higher open rate than emails.
4.  **Frequent and Repeated Use:** If a customer uses your service daily (e.g., banking, fitness, social networks), an app is more convenient than constantly typing a URL.

## Real-World Example
Loyalty program. Cards in the wallet are a thing of the past. An app where the customer sees their points, discounts, and receives notifications about promotions builds loyalty much more effectively.

An app is not a replacement for a website. It is the next step in the evolution of your digital business.
