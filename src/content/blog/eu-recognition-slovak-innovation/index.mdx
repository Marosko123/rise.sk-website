## A New Era for Slovak Research

Yesterday, November 20, 2025, the European Commission officially recognized the steps taken by the Slovak Republic in supporting science and research. In the latest report on the competitiveness of member states, Slovakia was labeled the "jumper of the year" in the Central European region.

### From Assembly Line to Innovation Hub

For years, we have been known as the "Detroit of Europe" due to our strong focus on car manufacturing. However, the report highlights a key shift – massive investments in **R&D (Research & Development)** centers directly adjacent to production plants.

It's no longer just about assembling cars, but about development:
*   **Autonomous Driving Systems:** New laboratories in Žilina and Bratislava.
*   **Electromobility:** Development of next-generation battery technologies in the Považie region.

### What Does This Mean for the IT Sector?

For us at **Rise.sk** and the entire developer community, this is a clear signal. The demand for specialists is changing. It is no longer enough to just know how to code websites. The market is starving for experts in:
1.  **Embedded Systems** (C/C++, Rust)
2.  **Machine Learning and AI** for real-time image processing
3.  **Big Data Analysis** from vehicle telemetry

> "Investments in science are not just numbers in a budget. They are new high-value-added jobs that will keep talent at home," stated the EC President during the press conference.

### Challenges and Opportunities

Although funding is secured (we are talking about a package exceeding €2.5 billion for the next 3 years), human capital remains a challenge. Cooperation between universities and the private sector will be crucial.

Slovakia has a chance to become a leader in **Automotive AI**. It is up to us, the developers, to seize this opportunity.
